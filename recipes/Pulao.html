Pulao

Description

Many historians believe that Pulao originated from Persia and was brought to India by the Mughals. Biryani was further developed in the Mughal royal kitchen.

Sindhi pulao developed in the Sindh region of what is now Pakistan, and Hyderabadi biryani developed in the city of Hyderabad in South India.
